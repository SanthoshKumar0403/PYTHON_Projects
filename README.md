## üìà Key Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) Insights

The analysis focused on identifying the highest-value customer segments by analyzing spend and order volume across various demographic factors.

### 1. Sales by Gender & Marital Status

The data clearly indicates that **Female customers** drive significantly higher sales and order volume compared to male customers. Furthermore, **Married women** are the dominant spending group. This highlights the importance of targeting products and advertising toward married female consumers.



### 2. Top Performing Geographies

The analysis of sales by state revealed strong regional preferences, which is critical for logistics and state-specific marketing campaigns.

* **Top 3 States by Sales:** **Uttar Pradesh (UP), Maharashtra, and Karnataka** account for the majority of the total sales amount.
* **Recommendation:** Marketing resources and inventory stock should be heavily concentrated in these three high-value states.



### 3. High-Value Product Categories

Understanding which product categories drive revenue helps in inventory management and seasonal stocking decisions.

* **Top 3 Product Categories by Orders:** **Food, Clothing & Apparel, and Electronics**.
* These categories represent the highest frequency of purchases during the Diwali season, confirming their status as essential festive items.
